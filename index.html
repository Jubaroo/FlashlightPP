Privacy Policy for Flashlight App

Effective Date: 02/11/2025

Last Updated: 02/11/2025

1. Information We Collect

a. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

b. Permissions & Local Storage

  • The App requires Camera Permission to control the flashlight. We do not access your camera feed.
  • We use SharedPreferences to store user preferences locally on your device. No data is uploaded.

c. AdMob (Advertising)

The App displays ads via Google AdMob, which may collect certain device information for ad personalization.

For more details, refer to: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any user data, we only use locally stored settings to:

  • Control flashlight operation
  • Save user preferences (e.g., brightness level, ad-free status)

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

  • AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data for ad personalization.
  • Manage ad preferences here: Google Ads Settings

Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

  • If you purchase "Remove Ads", payment is processed via Google Play. We do not store any payment details.

4. User Choices & Controls

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: Manage settings here: Google Ad Preferences
  • Ad Removal: Users may opt for an ad-free experience through an in-app purchase.

5. Security

Since we do not store or process user data, security risks are minimized.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 17. AdMob may serve interest-based ads, so parental guidance is recommended.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notice.
